About Me

I am Fangqing Yuan, recently graduated from University of Michigan, Ann Arbor with a Master in Data Science. I received my Bachelor degree at LUISS Guido Carli, Rome, Italy, where I studied Management and Computer Science and had spent one semester at KU Leuven, Belgium.

I am actively looking for jobs. I am open to positions in Data Science, Data Analytics, Data Engineering, Software Engineering, and similar positions longing for my Statistics and Programming background.

I am currently based in Ann Arbor, Michigan, but I am willing to move to anywhere in the world.

You may find my Resume/CV here. And my LinkedIn Profile.

I am interested in Time Series Analysis, Natural Language Processing, Causal Inference, Recommendation System, Survey Methodology, and all Data Science topics in general.

You may find my papers and projects here:

  • Assessing the Effects of Applying Ranking-Boosting Strategy on Friends Recommendation System on User Activeness on Kuaishou Short-Video Social Network Platform (Master Program Capstone Project Paper)
  • Regression analysis of contract bridge hand evaluation and its application on bidding (Bachelor Degree Thesis)
  • Analysis of Middle-East Respiratory Syndrome coronavirus in Saudi Arabia
  • Visualization and vaccination analysis computer virus propagation based on Sublinear Preferential Attachment Network
  • Analysis of Battery-Powered Electric Vehicles Sales in the United States

More About Me

I am decorated Bridge player. Having started learning bridge since 2011, I participated in countless tournaments and achieved multiple awards. My biggest awards include China U21 Champion in 2017, Runner-up in Junior group at Asian Bridge Congress, and twice the 3rd place in Junior United States Bridge Championship in 2022 and 2024. I also represented China U21 Team in 2016 World Youth Bridge Team Championship.

Bridge is a life-long passion for me that sharpens my fast-paced real-time analysis, decision making, and game strategies. It gives me invaluable treasure of Partnership, Teamwork, and Friendship.

I am also passionate about using data science method on improving bridge bidding and playing strategies. My Bachelor thesis was on improving hand evaluation method by regression analysis.

I love music, especially pop songs and ballads in Mainland China, Hong Kong, and Taiwan.

I am a die-hard fan of AC Milan, Forza Milan! I enjoy watching European football in general.

I follow Snooker closely, and am fan of Si Jiahui. I also enjoy applying analytics on sports, here is an example on snooker.

I love reading. My favorite authors are Yu Hua and Italo Calvino.

I am huge fan of Paradox Interactive grand strategy games, especially HOI4 and EU4, which intensified my interest in history in general. I like explore alternative history scenarios, in particular Kaiserreich, which I wrote about here.
