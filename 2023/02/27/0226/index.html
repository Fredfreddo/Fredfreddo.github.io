The last, I’d say, 100 days, are a somehow weird and tough period of my life.

Not being sure what was the trigger, I went through a long time of being lost and depressed. As for the outcome, I decided to live a special semester, and I just broke up.

I could be seen as a gesture of cutting the old life behind. The relationship lasted about three years and three months, which includes most of my undergraduate life in Europe, and then here, and it was almost all her whole college life.

Last night Munger was out of wireless internet, so I was lying on bed going through the pictures on my phone. Glorious days, indeed, though somehow attributed to those beautiful and mind-freshening views of Italia.

We had a close relationship, and we both had a rather petite life circle due to covid and our personalities. What I wanted to say, is , that during those years together and apart, our passions and likes were developing and changing.

I had no interest Kaiserreich, Habsburg, European history in general before I left Italy. I couldn’t care less when I saw some Spanish-related institutes in Leuven. I only started regretting not seeing enough European cities while I have been in state for a few months.

I have no certain plan right now. Make sure my status is ok, read all the books I currently have, play some good bridge, travel a bit, and cure, try figure thing with my family.

And this, is the extra plan. I want to write.

It’d be like the songs I wrote about on WeChat before. Maybe a few time a week, I’d try let some stories happen to me everyday and write them down. And there are lots of books I am reading worth putting my thought into solid words rather than some fantasies that both intrigue and bother me ever since growing up.

So, I’d start on some of my new stories, hope they will be coming soon.

Buona Giornata.

Fred.
